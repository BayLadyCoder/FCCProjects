Taylor Swift's Timeline

  • December 13th 1989 - Taylor Swift was born in Reading, Pennsylvania
  • 2004 - Swift moved to Nashville, Tennessee years olds
  • October 24th, 2006 - Taylor Swift first album was released
  • November 11th, 2008 - Fearless album was released
  • 2009 - The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 Album Chart with sales of 592,304, and has since sold over 8.6 million copies worldwide
  • 2009 - Swift became the first country music artist to win an MTV Video Music Award when "You Belong with Me" was named Best Female Video in 2009
  • 2010 - Swift won four Grammy Awards. She was the youngest ever artist to win Album of the Year
  • October 25th, 2010 - Swift released her third studio album, Speak Now. She wrote all 14 songs alone
  • October 22nd, 2012 - Swift's fourth studio album, Red, was released.
  • May 2013 - Red had sold over 6 million copies
  • October 27th, 2014 - Swift's fifth studio album, 1989, was released. Swift herself described 1989 as her first "official" pop release
  • February 2015 - 1989 had sold over 8.6 million copies worldwide.
